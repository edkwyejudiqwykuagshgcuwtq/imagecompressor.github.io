Frequently Asked Questions

Are my files uploaded to your servers?

No! All processing happens directly in your browser. Your files never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy.

Is there a limit to file sizes I can process?

Since processing happens in your browser, limits depend on your device's memory. For best results, we recommend files under 50MB each.

Do I need to create an account?

No accounts needed! All tools are available immediately with no registration required.

Can I use these tools on my phone?

Absolutely! Our tools are fully responsive and work perfectly on mobile devices.

Are there any hidden costs?

No, all tools are completely free to use with no hidden fees or premium versions.
